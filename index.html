Key Amazon FBA Shipment Guidelines

Carton Limits

Individual cartons must not exceed 25 inches on any side or weigh more than 50 pounds.

Pallet Dimensions

Use 40" x 48" pallets, not to exceed 72" in height or 1,500 lbs in weight.

Oversized Products

A single item is oversized if it is over 20 lbs or has any side longer than 18 inches.
